📋 Quick Care Tips

🌾

Hay Every Day

Rabbits and guinea pigs need unlimited hay – it keeps their digestive system healthy and wears down their teeth.

💧

Fresh Water

Always provide clean, fresh water. Change it every day and clean the bottle or bowl regularly.

🏠

Safe Space

Every pet needs a safe, quiet space to retreat to when they feel scared or overwhelmed.

🤝

Go Slowly

When you first get a pet, let them settle in for a few days before handling. Let them come to you!

🩺

Vet Visits

Book a vet check-up within the first week. Annual check-ups keep your pet healthy long-term.

🎾

Play Time

All pets need mental stimulation! Toys, tunnels, and supervised exploration keep them happy.
